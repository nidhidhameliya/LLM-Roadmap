# Career Guide

## Landing an AI Engineering Role
1. **Portfolio over Certificates:** A working RAG application deployed on the web is worth 10 online certificates. Build things.
2. **Open Source:** Contribute to LangChain, LlamaIndex, or Hugging Face libraries. Even documentation fixes get your name out there.
3. **Write:** Document your learnings. Write a blog post about how you built your mini-projects.

## Freelancing
- Many traditional businesses need simple AI integrations (e.g., an internal chat bot for their PDFs, or an automated email drafter).
- Start small. Offer to build a prototype for a fixed, low price to build your portfolio.
